# Nirvana Living Brand Standards

## Identity
- **Organization:** Nirvana Living Organization
- **Tagline:** Living Well is the Mission
- **Website:** NirvanaLiving.org
- **Location:** Asbury Park, NJ
- **Trademark:** Health Log HandyGuide® (IrisDesignHub LLC)

## Logo
- Primary logo file: NL_Logo_FLower.png
- Always display on white (#FFFFFF) or light backgrounds
- Never place on dark backgrounds without transparency treatment
- Footer logo: NL_Logo_Footer.png
- Favicon: NL_Flavicon.png

## Colors

### Primary Brand Colors
- **Deep Green:** #0E5000
- **Gold / Yellow:** #FFC700
- **Olive Green:** #859C2A
- **White:** #FFFFFF

### Site Block Colors
- **Olive Green:** #859C2A
- **Warm Tan:** #C4B06D
- **Cream:** #FAE9C0
- **Dark Forest Green:** #405834
- **Light Yellow-Green:** #BED26F

## Color Usage Guidelines
- **#0E5000** — primary brand green, headers, key UI elements
- **#FFC700** — accent gold, highlights, calls to action
- **#859C2A** — secondary green, section backgrounds, borders
- **#405834** — dark backgrounds, footer, navigation
- **#FAE9C0** — light cream backgrounds, cards
- **#BED26F** — light green accents, hover states
- **#C4B06D** — warm tan, secondary accents
- **#FFFFFF** — white backgrounds, logo field, clean layouts

## Typography
- **Display / Headers:** Cormorant Garamond (serif)
- **Body / UI:** DM Sans (sans-serif)
- **Source:** Google Fonts

## Voice & Tone
- Warm, personal, inclusive
- Plain language — no jargon
- Empowering not prescriptive
- Always: "All are welcome on the journey"

## App Header Standard
- White (#FFFFFF) background
- Deep green (#0E5000) bottom border
- Logo top left (NL_Logo_FLower.png on white)
- App title in Deep Green (#0E5000), Cormorant Garamond
- Subtitle in Olive Green (#859C2A), DM Sans

## Footer Standard
- Dark Forest Green (#405834) background
- Left: Health Log HandyGuide® + app subtitle in Gold (#FFC700)
- Right: Month Year · Nirvana Living Organization in White (#FFFFFF)
- Bottom line: trademark notice + share permission in Light Green (#BED26F)

## Four Pillars (always in this order)
Body • Mind • Emotion • Spirit
